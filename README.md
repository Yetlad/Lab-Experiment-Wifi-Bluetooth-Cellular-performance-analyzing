## Lab Experiment: Wifi, Bluetooth, Cellular performance analyzing

## Observations & Comparative Analysis
 1. Received Power

Decreases exponentially with distance for all systems.

Cellular maintains usable power over much longer distances.

Bluetooth drops quickly due to low transmit power.

 2. SINR

Closely follows received power trend.

WiFi maintains high SINR at short distances.

Bluetooth SINR degrades rapidly.

Cellular SINR remains stable over kilometers due to high transmit power.

 3. Channel Capacity

WiFi achieves highest capacity due to large bandwidth (22 MHz).

Bluetooth capacity is moderate (2 MHz bandwidth).

Cellular capacity is lowest due to narrow bandwidth (200 kHz), even though it covers longer distances.

## Key Trend Summary
Technology	Range	Bandwidth	Capacity	Best Use Case
WiFi	Medium	High	Very High	Internet access
Bluetooth	Short	Medium	Moderate	Personal devices
Cellular	Very Long	Low	Low–Moderate	Wide area coverage
